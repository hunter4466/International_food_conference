INTERNATIONAL FOOD CONFERENCE 2021

The international food conference bring the best chefs around the world to tell us about their experience, knowledge and techniques. This space is for all the people in the gastronomic industry, chefs, food and beverage managers, restaurant owners and students. This year we have prepared a special program for every people, we have lectures, exhibitions, forum, workshop and the event special feature, networking, swim deep into an endless line of people looking for someone like you with your talent, talk about job offers, techniques and more.
